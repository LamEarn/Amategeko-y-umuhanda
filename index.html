The minimum transaction amount is 5 USDT. There is no strict maximum, but for large amounts (over 1,000 USDT) please contact admin in advance.

For buying USDT, we accept MTN Mobile Money (MoMo). For selling USDT, we support BEP20, TRC20, and ERC20 networks.

Rates are set by admin based on the global USDT market price. Rates are typically updated daily or during significant market movements.
Is this service safe and legitimate? ▼
Yes. Smart Crypto Exchange Rwanda has served hundreds of customers. We provide personal admin support and transparent transactions. Always use our official contacts only.
Can I trade at night or on weekends? ▼
